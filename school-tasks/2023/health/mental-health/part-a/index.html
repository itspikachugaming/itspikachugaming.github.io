Exploring Youth Statistics on Mental Health

Part A

 

Understanding mental health

Common mental health concerns for young people are anxiety, depression, eating disorders, drug and alcohol use, feeling unsafe, discrimination, and bullying.
Examining relevant statistics and trends.
  • Around 19% of our population will experience some form of mental illness or disorder. Of this 19%, most 12% will experience illnesses such as depression or anxiety and return to good mental health in time.
  • Around 11% reported ever having deliberately injured themselves.
  • Around 7.5% had seriously considered attempting suicide
Services
 
There are many free mental health hotlines and mental health online support services across Victoria, which provide crisis support, information, resources, counselling and therapy to people of all ages.

 

Services include (but not limited to): Beyondblue, Mensline Australia, Anxiety Recovery Centre Victoria (ARCVic), Direct Line and Relationships Australia
