FAQ


Q. What is TimeGuard?

A. TimeGuard is a simple but powerful app that can help you hide distractions like social media so you can focus on what you need to get done. It seamlessly intergrates with your iPhone's screen time and a to-do list of your liking!


Q. How much is TimeGuard?

A. TimeGuard is free for everyone! Although we have a subscription for A$0.99/month to get rid of all of the ads, no feature will be locked behind a paywall!
